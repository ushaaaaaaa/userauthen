My Trip Itinerary

Day 1 - Arrival and Central Paris

  • Morning: Arrive, check-in, breakfast at a local café.
  • Late Morning: Visit the Louvre Museum
  • Afternoon: Lunch near Louvre, stroll through Tuileries Garden, visit Place de la Concorde
  • Evening: Walk along Champs-Élysées, visit Arc de Triomphe, dinner on Champs-Élysées.

Day 2 - Iconic Landmarks

  • Morning: Breakfast, visit Eiffel Tower.
  • Late Morning: Seine River Cruise.
  • Afternoon: Lunch, visit Notre Dame Cathedral.
  • Evening: Explore Latin Quarter, dinner in Latin Quarter

Day 3 - Art and Culture

  • Morning: Breakfast, visit Musée d’Orsay.
  • Late Morning:Head to Montmartre, visit Sacré-Cœur Basilica..
  • Afternoon: Lunch in Montmartre, explore artistic streets..
  • Evening: Optional Moulin Rouge show, dinner in Montmartre.
